  1832→Operational readiness ensures that the RxBD team has the tools,

  1837→[]{#_Toc100068 .anchor}**11.1 Support Strategy**
  1838→
  1839→RxBD implements a tiered support model aligned with the subscription
  1840→tiers. Free-tier doctors receive support through a community forum
  1841→(built with GitHub Discussions for zero cost) and email support with a
  1842→72-hour response SLA. Premium doctors receive priority support via
  1843→WhatsApp Business API and email with a 24-hour response SLA.
  1844→
  1845→The WhatsApp Business API integration is implemented using the WhatsApp
  1846→Business Platform (Cloud API, free tier: 1,000 conversations per month).
  1847→A dedicated business number is configured through Meta\'s Business
  1848→Manager. When a Premium doctor sends a WhatsApp message to the RxBD
  1849→support number, the message is received via webhook, logged in a support
  1850→tracking spreadsheet, and routed to the on-call support team member.
  1851→Responses are sent through the same WhatsApp channel, maintaining a
  1852→conversational context. For the launch phase, the support team consists
  1853→of the founding members, with a plan to hire a dedicated support agent
  1854→once Premium subscriptions exceed 50 active users.
  1855→
  1856→[]{#_Toc100069 .anchor}**11.2 Beta Feedback Loop**
  1857→
  1858→The beta program targets 3 to 5 pilot doctors selected through direct
  1859→outreach to the founding team\'s professional network. Selection
  1860→criteria include: active private chamber practice with a minimum of 20
  1861→patients per day, willingness to provide structured feedback via a
  1862→dedicated Google Form after each session, representation of at least 2
  1863→different medical specialties (to test specialty-specific prescription
  1864→patterns), and varying levels of technical literacy (from tech-savvy to
  1865→minimal computer experience).
  1866→
  1867→Feedback is collected through three channels: a structured Google Form
  1868→distributed after each day of use (covering ease of use, search
  1869→accuracy, PDF quality, and feature requests), a dedicated WhatsApp group
  1870→for real-time issues and suggestions, and automated analytics tracking
  1871→of key usage metrics. The feedback is reviewed weekly by the founding
  1872→team, prioritized using a RICE (Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort)
  1873→framework, and incorporated into the next sprint. A centralized feedback
  1874→tracker spreadsheet maintains a public changelog visible to all beta
  1875→participants, ensuring transparency and demonstrating responsiveness.

  2042→[]{#_Toc100075 .anchor}**13. Support FAQ**
  2043→
  2044→This section provides answers to the most frequently asked questions,
  2045→organized by topic. These answers are also available on the RxBD website
  2046→at rxbd.com.bd/faq.
  2047→
  2048→[]{#_Toc100076 .anchor}**13.1 Getting Started**
  2049→
  2050→-   **Is RxBD truly free to use?** Yes. The core features of RxBD are
  2051→    completely free for up to 50 prescriptions per month. This limit
  2052→    resets automatically every 30 days. No credit card is required to
  2053→    sign up, and there are no hidden fees or trial periods that expire.
  2054→
  2055→-   **Do I need to install any software?** No. RxBD is a web-based
  2056→    application accessible from any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox,
  2057→    Safari, Edge) on desktop, tablet, or phone. There is nothing to
  2058→    download or install. An offline-capable Progressive Web App (PWA)
  2059→    version is planned for future release.
  2060→
  2061→-   **How do I get started with my first prescription?** After signing
  2062→    up with your email, complete your profile on the Settings page
  2063→    (name, degrees, specialty, chamber information). The guided
  2064→    onboarding walkthrough will then help you write a test prescription
  2065→    with a dummy patient. The entire setup takes less than 5 minutes.
  2066→
  2067→-   **Is RxBD available in Bengali?** Yes. RxBD supports both English
  2068→    and Bengali (Bangla) interfaces. The language is automatically
  2069→    detected from your browser settings, and you can also switch
  2070→    manually using the language toggle in the navigation bar.
  2071→
  2072→[]{#_Toc100077 .anchor}**13.2 Prescriptions & Usage**
  2073→
  2074→-   **What happens if my internet connection is slow or drops?** RxBD
  2075→    has a Local-First auto-save feature that persists your draft
  2076→    prescription to your browser every 10 seconds. If you lose
  2077→    connectivity, your work is safe. When you reconnect, RxBD will
  2078→    detect your local draft and prompt you to resume exactly where you
  2079→    left off.
  2080→
  2081→-   **How can I verify if a prescription is authentic?** Every
  2082→    prescription PDF includes a unique QR code in the footer. Scanning
  2083→    the QR code with any smartphone camera opens the official RxBD
  2084→    verification page, which displays the doctor\'s name, patient\'s
  2085→    name, visit date, and a verified status indicator. This ensures the
  2086→    prescription has not been tampered with.
  2087→
  2088→-   **Can I save my frequent medicine lists?** Yes. Premium plan users
  2089→    can create Prescription Templates (Macros) for common conditions
  2090→    like Gastritis, Flu, or Cold. A Macro saves your entire medication
  2091→    set (with dosages and instructions) so you can load it with a single
  2092→    click during a consultation, saving significant time during
  2093→    high-volume sessions.
  2094→
  2095→-   **What prescription formats are available?** RxBD generates
  2096→    prescriptions in PDF format that can be downloaded, printed on any
  2097→    standard A4 printer, or shared digitally. The print layout follows
  2098→    the standard Bangladesh medical prescription format that pharmacies
  2099→    expect.
  2100→
  2101→[]{#_Toc100078 .anchor}**13.3 Security & Privacy**
  2102→
  2103→-   **Is my patient data private and secure?** Yes. RxBD uses Row Level
  2104→    Security (RLS) at the database level to ensure that you have
  2105→    exclusive access to your patient records. No other doctor, nor even
  2106→    the RxBD team, can view your patient data. All data is encrypted
  2107→    both at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). RxBD maintains a
  2108→    strict policy against selling or sharing patient or doctor data with
  2109→    third parties.
  2110→
  2111→-   **Can I export my patient records?** Yes. You can export your entire
  2112→    patient list and prescription history from the Settings page.
  2113→    Free-tier users can export in CSV format, and Premium users can
  2114→    additionally export in JSON format for migration to other EMR
  2115→    systems.
  2116→
  2117→-   **What happens to my data if I delete my account?** When you delete
  2118→    your account, a 30-day grace period allows you to change your mind
  2119→    and restore it. After 30 days, all your data (profile, patients,
  2120→    prescriptions, uploaded files) is permanently deleted from our
  2121→    servers. Anonymized audit records are retained for 7 years as
  2122→    required by law.
  2123→
  2124→[]{#_Toc100079 .anchor}**13.4 Billing & Subscription**
  2125→
  2126→-   **What is the difference between Free and Premium?** The Free plan
  2127→    includes 50 prescriptions per month with the standard PDF template.
  2128→    The Premium plan (BDT 499/month) unlocks unlimited prescriptions,
  2129→    custom letterhead branding with your logo and colors, digital
  2130→    signature embedding, Prescription Templates (Macros), full patient
  2131→    history, JSON data export, and priority WhatsApp support.
  2132→
  2133→-   **How do I pay for the Premium plan?** We use SSLCommerz,
  2134→    Bangladesh\'s leading payment gateway, which supports bKash, Nagad,
  2135→    Rocket, DBBL Nexus, and Visa/Mastercard. You can pay directly from
  2136→    the upgrade page without leaving the application.
  2137→
  2138→-   **Will I lose my data if my Premium subscription expires?** No. All
  2139→    your data remains fully accessible even after your Premium
  2140→    subscription expires. You simply revert to the Free tier with a
  2141→    50-prescription monthly limit. Your custom letterhead and digital
  2142→    signature settings are preserved and will reactivate if you
  2143→    resubscribe.
